Job Openings

Accounts Manager
Overview:

We are seeking an experienced Accounts Manager with 6-10 years of experience in accounting and auditing.

Responsibilities:
  • Oversee daily accounting operations, including accounts payable/receivable, general ledger, and bank reconciliations.
  • Prepare and analyze financial statements, ensuring accuracy and compliance with relevant regulations.
  • Conduct audits and reviews to ensure financial integrity and compliance.
  • Manage budgeting, forecasting, and financial planning processes.
  • Coordinate with external auditors and regulatory bodies as required.
  • Assist in strategic planning and business development initiatives.
Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field. A CPA or equivalent professional certification is preferred.
  • 6-10 years of experience in accounting and auditing, preferably within an accounting and auditing firm.
  • Proven experience in managing a team and overseeing financial operations.
Required Skills:
  • Strong knowledge of accounting principles, standards, and regulations.
  • Proficiency in accounting software and ERP systems.
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office, especially Excel.
