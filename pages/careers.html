Job Openings

Accounts Manager
Overview:

We are seeking an experienced Accounts Manager with 6-10 years of experience in accounting and auditing.

Responsibilities:
  • Oversee daily accounting operations, including accounts payable/receivable, general ledger, and bank reconciliations.
  • Prepare and analyze financial statements, ensuring accuracy and compliance with relevant regulations.
  • Conduct audits and reviews to ensure financial integrity and compliance.
  • Manage budgeting, forecasting, and financial planning processes.
  • Coordinate with external auditors and regulatory bodies as required.
  • Assist in strategic planning and business development initiatives.
Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field. A CPA or equivalent professional certification is preferred.
  • 6-10 years of experience in accounting and auditing, preferably within an accounting and auditing firm.
  • Proven experience in managing a team and overseeing financial operations.
Required Skills:
  • Strong knowledge of accounting principles, standards, and regulations.
  • Proficiency in accounting software and ERP systems.
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office, especially Excel.
Content Writer
Overview:

We are looking for a talented Content Writer to join our team. The ideal candidate will be responsible for creating compelling, engaging, and SEO-friendly content across various platforms.

Responsibilities:
  • Research and write high-quality content for blogs, articles, social media, website copy, and other digital platforms.
  • Develop content that aligns with the company’s brand voice and marketing goals.
  • Conduct keyword research and use SEO best practices to optimize content.
  • Collaborate with the marketing team to create content strategies.
  • Edit and proofread content to ensure accuracy and consistency.
  • Stay up-to-date with industry trends and incorporate relevant changes into content creation.
Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree in English, Communications, Journalism, Marketing, or a related field.
  • Proven experience as a Content Writer, Copywriter, or similar role.
  • Strong portfolio of published articles or content pieces.
Required Skills:
  • Proficiency in SEO and understanding of content optimization.
  • Exceptional writing, editing, and proofreading skills.
  • Strong research skills to ensure content accuracy and relevance.
  • Excellent time management and organizational skills.
Sales Executive
Overview:

We are looking for a dynamic Sales Executive with 2-3 years of real-time experience in sales and marketing. The ideal candidate will excel in generating leads, closing deals, and fostering long-term customer relationships.

Responsibilities:
  • Identify and target potential clients through research, networking, and cold calling.
  • Develop and execute sales strategies to meet or exceed sales targets.
  • Present and demonstrate products or services to potential clients.
  • Collaborate with the marketing team to develop promotional materials and campaigns.
  • Maintain accurate records of sales activities, customer interactions, and sales pipeline.
  • Provide regular reports on sales performance and market feedback to management.
Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree in Business, Marketing, or a related field is preferred.
  • 2-3 years of real-time experience in sales and marketing, preferably in the relevant industry.
  • Proven track record of meeting or exceeding sales targets.
Required Skills:
  • Strong communication, negotiation, and interpersonal skills.
  • Excellent presentation and demonstration skills.
  • Proficiency in using CRM software and Microsoft Office.
  • Ability to build and maintain strong relationships with clients.
